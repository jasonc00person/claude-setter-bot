In ManyChat Live Chat, left-aligned (gray) messages are ALWAYS from the prospect. Right-aligned (blue) messages are ALWAYS from Jason (us).

**Why:** Made this mistake multiple times — read Jason's own replies as if they were from the prospect, almost double-sent messages and misidentified conversation states.

**How to apply:** Before responding to any ManyChat conversation, verify who sent the last message by checking alignment. Left = them, Right = us. If the last message is right-aligned, that means WE sent it and are waiting for their reply — do NOT send another message (no double texting).
