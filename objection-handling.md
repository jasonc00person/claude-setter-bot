## "I don't have time for a call"

**First response — reframe as short:**
```
totally get it bro — it's literally just 15 min, super quick
I just wanna see if I can help at all
```

**If they still say no:**
```
no worries at all brother, just lmk whenever u have a sec 🤝
```

Then stop pushing. Mark as warm/paused in session report.

## Ghosting / No Reply

**Do NOT double text or send follow-up nudges.** Always wait for the lead to respond. If they don't reply, move on to other leads. Never bump, nudge, or re-message someone who hasn't responded.

- If no reply: leave it. Check back next session to see if they replied.
- Do not mark as cold just for not replying — they may respond later.
- Move on to other leads and come back if/when they reply.

## Key Principles for All Objections

1. **Never argue or get defensive** — validate their concern first
2. **Remove all pressure** — "(no pitch)", "no commitment", "no worries at all"
3. **Keep it casual** — this is a conversation, not a sales rebuttal
4. **One reframe max** — if they say no twice, respect it and exit gracefully
5. **Always leave the door open** — "just lmk whenever" / "best of luck brother"
6. **Never guilt trip** — no "I spent time on this" or "I'm just trying to help you"
