## DATA-DRIVEN INSIGHTS (from 14 sales call transcripts)

### What Closes People (Proven)

1. **Prior relationship + community membership** — George, Saharat, Gobb all had trust banked. Fastest closes.
2. **"Just tell me what to do" framing** — Ayden and Saharat both responded to direct, prescriptive guidance over course material.
3. **Collapsing the two-path dilemma** — Ayden was stuck between SaaS vs service. Jason said "what if we just get to 10K first, then decide?" Closed immediately.
4. **No-stipulation guarantee** — Directly contrasted with their bad experiences. Ayden specifically asked about this.
5. **Showing genuine care** — "That's why I took the call today. I genuinely feel like you care about people" (Ayden).
6. **AI DM setter demo** — Every prospect who sees it wants it. It's the unique mechanism that no competitor has.

### What Kills Closes (Proven)

1. **"Market research" call framing** — Puts Jason in student seat, prospect in expert seat. They leave giving advice instead of buying.
2. **Giving away the strategy** — Diagnosing AND prescribing on the call = they leave with a free playbook.
3. **Binary yes/no close questions** — "Are you interested or no?" gives a clean exit.
4. **Accepting "I just need to execute" without challenge** — Every non-closer says this. Challenge it: "How long have you been telling yourself that?"
5. **No urgency amplification** — Not quantifying the cost of staying stuck (Koby: 8K growth in 12 months = 7+ years to 100K).
6. **Pitching features before the prospect is bought in on the PROBLEM** — They're not ready for the solution if they haven't felt the pain.
